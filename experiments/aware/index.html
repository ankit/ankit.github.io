Supported Tasks in the Aware prototype


  • Instructor giving a quiz - instructor_quiz_1.html


    • Click on "Sign In"
    • Click on "Give a Quiz" button
    • Pick Quiz 3 and click "Give this Quiz" button
    • See Results and click on "Go Back to Slides"


  • Instructor answering student questions during a lecture - instructor_questions_1.html


    • Click on "Questions" button to view pending questions
    • Click on "Answer" to show and answer a question to the entire class
    • Click on "Go Back to Slides" to go back to slides


  • Student participating in forum - student_forum_1.html


    • Look at existing questions in forum
    • Type "a" in search textfield to show autocomplete
    • Click on "Add a new post" to post a new thread


  • Student during Lecture - student_questions_1.html


    • Current lecture slide is visible to the student
    • Ask a question by clicking on the "Ask a Question" button
    • Send Feedback by clicking on "Send Feedback" button
    • View slides fullscreen by clicking on "Go Fullscreen" button
    • Rate a slide by choosing number of stars
    • Click on "Questions" to view questions previously asked by students
    • Shows the previous questions asked by students during the lecture (both answered and pending)
